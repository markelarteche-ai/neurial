White noise is a consistent sound that covers all frequencies equally. It works by masking background noises that might otherwise wake you up or prevent you from falling asleep.

When your environment is quiet, your brain stays alert and sensitive to sudden sounds — a car outside, a door closing, a notification. White noise creates a steady audio blanket that neutralizes these spikes, keeping your brain in a relaxed state.

Studies show that white noise can reduce the time it takes to fall asleep by up to 40% in noisy environments. It also helps maintain sleep depth throughout the night by preventing micro-awakenings caused by environmental sounds.

Neurial lets you mix white noise with nature layers like rain or ocean waves, and pair it with delta brainwave entrainment — the frequency your brain operates at during deep sleep. This combination is one of the most effective audio setups for sleep available.
